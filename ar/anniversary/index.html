The Genesis

BARDES was founded on 12 February 2018 under a strong commitment of brotherhood. Born from laughter in vocational high school corridors, 11 friends promised to maintain their bonds across space and time.

We grew not as a rigid formal organization, but as a safe digital and physical space for those bound by the same history. A sincere bond: Gather, Once, Forever.
